RZBiK (a playful pun on Żbik, or Lynx in Polish) was one of the two PEGaZ rockets at Józef Poniatowski High School.

Two of the test flights ended in RUDs (Rapid Unplanned Dissasemblies) because of off-the-shelf motor failures.
