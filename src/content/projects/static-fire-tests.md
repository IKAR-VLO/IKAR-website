Before launching any rocket, the motor must undergo static testing on the ground. We conducted two tests on our own test stand, and then a test of the WiWERNA Mk2E (Extended) variant on WITU's (Military Institute's of Armament Technology) facility.

These experiments let us prove the reliability of the design.
